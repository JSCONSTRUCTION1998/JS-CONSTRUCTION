About JS Construction

Founded in 1998 by Mr. Vinay Tomar, JS Construction has grown from a small local builder into a trusted construction partner across Mumbai. Our philosophy combines practical engineering, honest communication, and a commitment to delivering long-term value. We handle projects of all sizes — residential towers, commercial fit-outs, industrial facilities, and bespoke private homes.

Our approach

We begin every project with thorough planning and a transparent quote. Our site teams use systematic quality checklists and we maintain strict safety and compliance standards. Regular client site visits and weekly progress reports ensure you always know exactly where your project stands.

Core commitments

  • Clear communication and professional project management
  • Responsible sourcing and responsible disposal of construction waste
  • Skilled trades, supervised workmanship and certified materials

Meet the owner — Vinay Tomar

Mr. Vinay Tomar, the founder and driving force behind JS Construction, began his career in the late 1980s as a site engineer and worked his way up through hands-on experience. With a deep understanding of construction fundamentals and a practical approach to problem solving, Mr. Tomar has overseen hundreds of projects across the Mumbai metropolitan region. He is personally involved in major project decisions and client coordination, ensuring every project reflects his standard for reliability and quality.
